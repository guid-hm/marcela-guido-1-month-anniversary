what we're making

beet pasta · protein tbd

three servings — if it comes out super good, you can keep the extra two servings for another day. if its ehhh, you can give them to your mom and nala

Ingredients

ready for pickup on day of event.
  • Pasta (Rigatoni)½ lb · 8 oz
  • Raw beets4 oz, peeled & cubed
  • Full-fat coconut milk6 tbsp
  • Low-sodium veg broth¼ cup
  • Lemon juice1 tbsp
  • Garlic1–2 cloves
  • Nutritional yeast1 tbsp · opt.
  • Salt¼ tsp
  • Black pepperto taste
  • Reserved pasta water6 tbsp
  • Crumbled vegan fetatopping · opt.

Instructions

10 steps · about thirty minutes (totally guessing on the time. i honestly have no clue)
  1. 1 Cook the pasta to package directions. Before draining, reserve 6 tbsp of pasta water. Drain and set the pasta aside.
  2. 2 In a high-speed blender, combine the beets, coconut milk, broth, lemon juice, garlic, nutritional yeast, salt & pepper.
  3. 3 Blend 30 seconds to 1 minute, until smooth and creamy. Taste. Adjust salt or lemon to your liking.
  4. 4 Pour the sauce into the same pot you used for the pasta. Add the reserved pasta water.
  5. 5 Bring to a simmer over medium-low. Cook ~10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened.
  6. 6 Add the cooked pasta back to the pot and stir to coat every piece in pink.
  7. 7 Cook another 2–3 minutes until the sauce clings to the pasta.
  8. 8 This should honestly be step 1, but we're freestyling this. Cook your protein as desired.
  9. 9 Add protein to the pasta. Make sure to mix well until the protein is pink too.
  10. 10 Serve warm, topped with crumbled feta, parsley, & a candle on the table. (candle is optional)
